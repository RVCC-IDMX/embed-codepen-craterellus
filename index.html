Pen of the Week

Pen: Parallax Scroll Animation

by Isladjan

In this pen, the author has created an animation with HTML and CSS. The animation takes input from the user-- it moves as the user scrolls. The visuals are beautiful. The author makes use of a selective palette, yellow, orange and purple, in order to create a sense of visual harmony. They also vary contrast and saturation in order to create focal points as well as a sense of depth. I personally didn't know you could create something like this. I can't wait to start incorporation different types of animation into my projects.

See the Pen Parallax scroll animation by isladjan (@isladjan) on CodePen.
